Our platform supports an authority to collect revenue using this.

Web portal

Citizens are able to create unique profiles on the authority's official web portal. This provides a breakdown to all digitized services that citizens can pay online.

Mobile app

Citizens will download the government services payment app which will contain all digitized services e.g business permits, parking,land rates and rents.

Hand-held devices

Our handheld devices will support collection of revenues e.g parking, bus parks, markets, ferry etc. They will also be able to issue receipts, licenses and permits on the spot.

Banks and agents

We will provide our platform to banks and agents for citizens to pay government fees through their channels e.g branches, mobile banking, internet banking etc.

Revenue Management

RevenueSure enables a revenue authority to manage all revenue activities by providing a single point of view for all services offered and payments received by the County. All payments will be automatically reconcilled with government records where available. It also provides analytics with insights on trends, patterns and provisions of County services. This includes statistics on users and their requests.
